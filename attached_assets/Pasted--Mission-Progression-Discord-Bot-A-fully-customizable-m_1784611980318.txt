# Mission & Progression Discord Bot

A fully customizable mission system designed to keep your community active, organized, and rewarded.

## Mission Creation

Server Owners and Administrators can create missions directly from Discord using simple slash commands.

**Example:**
`/mission add <title> <description> <finer_details> <highest_rank_allowed (optional)> <lowest_rank_required>`

### Mission Fields

* **Title** – The mission's name.
* **Description** – A short summary displayed on the Mission Board.
* **Finer Details** – Extended instructions, objectives, rules, examples, or additional information sent to the player after claiming the mission.
* **Highest Rank Allowed (Optional)** – Restricts claiming to players at or below a specified rank. If left blank, there is no maximum rank restriction.
* **Lowest Rank Required** – The minimum progression rank required before a player may claim the mission.

Each mission is automatically posted to the designated **Mission Board** channel, where members can browse available objectives.

---

## Claiming Missions

Members can claim any available mission by pressing the **Accept Mission** button.

Once claimed:

* The mission is marked as **Claimed** on the Mission Board so others know it is currently being worked on.
* The bot sends the member a direct message containing the mission title, description, finer details, and any additional instructions.
* The bot begins tracking the mission session from the moment it is accepted.

If a member decides they no longer want to complete the mission, they may **Unaccept** it at any time. Doing so applies a small penalty of **-1 completed mission point**, discouraging players from claiming missions they don't intend to finish.

---

## Mission Submission

When a member finishes their mission, they simply reply to the bot's direct message with their proof, screenshots, videos, or any other required evidence.

After submitting their proof, they press the **Submit Mission** button.

The bot automatically compiles the entire mission history, including:

* Mission accepted timestamp
* Mission information
* Player responses
* Proof submitted
* Relevant conversation logs

This complete submission is then forwarded to a private **Mission Review** channel.

---

## Staff Review

Owners and Administrators review each submission inside the private review channel.

With a single button press they can either:

* ✅ **Accept** — The mission is successfully completed and rewards are granted.
* ❌ **Deny** — The submission is rejected and can optionally include a reason for the denial.

This creates a fast, organized moderation workflow while keeping mission discussions private.

---

## Rank Progression

Players earn permanent progression based on the number of **approved** missions they complete.

Example progression:

* **1** Completed Mission → Soldier
* **5** Completed Missions → Geared Soldier
* **10** Completed Missions → General
* **20** Completed Missions → Geared General
* **50** Completed Missions → Incomplete Yazzer
* **100** Completed Missions → TRUE YAZZER

Roles are awarded automatically as players reach each milestone.

---

## Statistics System

Every member has a personal statistics profile that tracks their progress across the server.

Tracked statistics include:

* Total completed missions
* Total accepted missions
* Total denied missions
* Missions currently in progress
* Missions abandoned
* Mission completion rate
* Current progression rank
* Highest rank achieved
* Date of first completed mission
* Date of most recent completed mission

### Rank-Based Mission Statistics

The bot also tracks how many completed missions a player has finished for each mission tier based on the mission's **Lowest Rank Required**.

For example:

* Soldier missions completed
* Geared Soldier missions completed
* General missions completed
* Geared General missions completed
* Incomplete Yazzer missions completed
* TRUE YAZZER missions completed

This allows players to see not only how many missions they've completed overall, but how many high-level missions they've conquered.

---

## Leaderboards

The bot includes server-wide leaderboards to encourage competition.

Leaderboard categories may include:

* Most Completed Missions
* Most High-Rank Missions Completed
* Highest Completion Rate
* Most Missions Completed This Week
* Most Missions Completed This Month
* Longest Completion Streak
* Fastest Average Mission Completion Time
* Highest Current Progression Rank

Leaderboards update automatically as missions are accepted and approved.
